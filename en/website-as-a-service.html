Website as a Service: your site as a subscription, not a one-off project

One annual fee covers a fast pure-code site, hosting, SSL, and ongoing positioning work. Optional performance component: 3% only on revenue from new clients we help you win — with transparent tracking.

Website as a Service (WaaS) means you buy an operating website as a service: we keep it fast, secure, and aligned with SEO and GEO best practices. That sits inside our broader RaaS (Revenue as a Service) model: the site is the foundation; acquisition and measurement are part of the same story.
