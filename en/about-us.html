About RaaS Automazioni

We are an Italian web agency built around a simple idea: we grow when you grow. Your site, SEO, GEO, and lead generation work together — with a transparent dashboard and pricing you can plan around.

What we do

RaaS (Revenue as a Service) means we deliver a fast, professional website in pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (no WordPress stack on your public site), then work on visibility on Google and in AI-assisted search. You pay a fixed annual fee for the Base (399 EUR + VAT/year) or E-commerce (599 EUR + VAT/year) package, plus 3% only on revenue from new clients we help you acquire — tracked in a way you can verify.

For the full company story and team details in Italian, see Chi siamo (Italian).

How we work with owners

Most of our clients are busy owners. You share goals and materials; we ship the site and publishing workflow. You do not run a complex builder on your side — we handle the technical layer and explain what matters for results.

Pure code

Lean static sites aimed at strong Core Web Vitals and clear structure for both search engines and AI citation.

Aligned incentives

The performance fee ties our work to revenue you can attribute to the channel — not to vanity metrics alone.

Transparency

Dashboard access, clear packages (399/599 + VAT/year), and the 3% rule documented up front.

Italy-first, English-ready

We serve Italian SMEs and work with clients who need English-facing pages and GEO for international visibility.
